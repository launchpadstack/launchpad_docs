Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 19, 2025
Effective Date: October 19, 2025

LaunchPad ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password (encrypted).
  • Profile Information: Optional information such as display name, country, and profile preferences.
  • User-Generated Content: Your progress data, answers to challenges, decisions made in side quests, and simulator game progress.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, concepts completed, challenge performance.
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, and performance data (via Sentry).

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and profile information you authorize.
  • Payment Information: Payment processing is handled by Google Play / Apple App Store. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain the App: To deliver app functionality, track your progress, and save your data.
  • Personalization: To customize your learning experience and recommend content.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and improve features.
  • Communication: To send you notifications about your progress, streaks, and app updates (if you opt-in).
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your questions and provide assistance.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: Progress data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Cloud Storage: Account data and sync information are stored on Supabase servers (located in the United States).
  • Error Tracking: Crash reports and error logs are sent to Sentry for debugging purposes.

3.2 How We Protect Your Data

  • Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 encryption before storage.
  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access.
  • Regular security audits and updates to address vulnerabilities.

Note: While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Sentry: Error tracking and crash reporting
  • Expo: App development and push notification infrastructure
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: Payment processing and in-app purchases

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

4.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where consent was the legal basis.
  • Object to Processing: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at launchpadstack@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

LaunchPad is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will delete such information from our systems.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App does not use cookies. However, we use the following tracking technologies:

  • Local Storage: To save your progress and preferences on your device.
  • Analytics: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the App.
  • Crash Reporting: Sentry collects crash data to help us fix bugs.

You can disable crash reporting in the App settings, but this may limit our ability to provide support.

8. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., social media sharing). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Progress Data: Retained until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Log Data: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or deleting your account in the App settings.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located in countries other than your own, including the United States. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
