Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Open the app and tap "Create Account". Enter your email, username, and password. You can also sign in with Google for faster registration.
What's the difference between Free and Premium?
Free Version: Access to 20 business concepts, 160 challenges, 20 side quests, and limited simulator stages.

LaunchPad Pro ($47.99/year): All 239 concepts, 1,912 challenges, 239 side quests, 150 simulator stages, Business Blueprint, advanced analytics, and ad-free experience.
Is LaunchPad suitable for beginners?
Yes! The app is designed for learners at all levels. Concepts start with basics and progressively build to advanced topics. The Year 9 reading level makes it accessible to everyone aged 13+.

Subscriptions & Payments

How do I subscribe to LaunchPad Pro?
1. Open the app and tap on any premium feature (marked with a crown icon)
2. Tap "Upgrade to Pro"
3. Choose your plan (Annual $47.99 or Monthly if available)
4. Complete payment through Google Play or Apple App Store
How do I cancel my subscription?
Android (Google Play):
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Tap Menu → Subscriptions
3. Find LaunchPad
4. Tap Cancel Subscription

iOS (App Store):
1. Open Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
2. Find LaunchPad
3. Tap Cancel Subscription
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Google Play or Apple App Store according to their refund policies. Generally, refunds are available within 48 hours of purchase if you haven't used the premium features. Contact the respective store's support for refund requests.
What payment methods are accepted?
All payment methods supported by Google Play (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS), including credit/debit cards, PayPal, carrier billing, and Google Play/App Store balance.

App Features

How does the Hearts/Lives system work?
You need 1 heart to attempt a challenge. You start with 5 hearts. If you answer incorrectly, you lose 1 heart. Hearts regenerate automatically: 1 heart every 20 minutes, up to a maximum of 5 hearts. You can also purchase 5 hearts for $0.99.
What is the Business Blueprint?
The Business Blueprint is an 8-step framework that guides you from idea validation to getting your first paying customers. It unlocks after completing concepts 1-20. Step 1 is free; steps 2-8 require LaunchPad Pro.
How do I earn money in the app?
You earn virtual money (in-app currency) by:
• Completing challenges correctly ($50 + $85 first-try bonus)
• Making correct decisions in side quests ($50 per decision)
• Progressing through simulator stages (varies by stage)

Use this money to unlock simulator stages and track your progress.
What is the Business Simulator?
The Business Simulator is a 150-stage interactive game where you make business decisions and build from poverty to $100M+ net worth. Each decision has real consequences that affect your progress. It's designed to apply the concepts you learn in a realistic scenario.

Progress & Data

Is my progress saved?
Yes! Your progress is automatically saved locally on your device. If you create an account and sign in, your progress also syncs to the cloud so you can access it from multiple devices.
Can I use LaunchPad offline?
Yes! Most features work offline. You can learn concepts, take challenges, and play the simulator without an internet connection. However, cloud sync, referrals, and leaderboards require an internet connection.
How do I reset my progress?
Currently, progress reset is not available directly in the app. If you need to reset your progress, please contact support at launchpadstack@gmail.com with your account email.
How do I delete my account?
1. Open the app
2. Go to Profile → Settings
3. Scroll to bottom and tap "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

Warning: This permanently deletes all your data and cannot be undone. Cancel your subscription first if you don't want to be charged.

Technical Issues

The app is crashing. What should I do?
Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest version from Google Play or App Store
4. Clear app cache (Android: Settings → Apps → LaunchPad → Clear Cache)
5. If issue persists, contact support with your device model and OS version
I'm not receiving notifications. How do I fix this?
Check App Permissions:
• Android: Settings → Apps → LaunchPad → Notifications (enable all)
• iOS: Settings → Notifications → LaunchPad (allow notifications)

Check In-App Settings:
Open app → Profile → Notification Settings (enable desired notifications)
The app is running slow. What can I do?
• Close other apps running in the background
• Free up device storage (at least 500MB recommended)
• Update the app to the latest version
• Restart your device
• If using an older device, performance may be limited

Account & Security

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
How do I change my email or username?
Currently, email and username changes must be done through support. Email us at launchpadstack@gmail.com with:
• Your current email/username

• Reason for the change
Is my data secure?
Yes! We take security seriously:

• We never sell your personal information
• See our Privacy Policy for complete details

Content & Learning

What age group is LaunchPad for?
LaunchPad is designed for ages 13 and older. The content uses Year 9 reading level (accessible to most teenagers and adults) but features adult business scenarios and professional examples.
How long does it take to complete all content?
It varies by learning pace:
• Casual learner (15 min/day): 6-9 months
• Regular learner (30 min/day): 3-6 months
• Intensive learner (1 hour/day): 2-3 months

The app is designed for steady, consistent learning rather than rushing through content.
